The Items in a Backyard

Golf Club

The golf club uses metal that was made for the space station. NASA needed stronger metal for space missions. The metal is stronger, softer, and stretchier.

Golf Ball

The Ben Hogan Company wanted to make new better golf balls. NASA helped by letting them use their high speed video technology. The golf company used the technology to study the ball in flight and made improvements.

Portable Electric Cooler

NASA needed to replace big parts used for refrigeration. They made coolers with small parts so they can fit in a small spacecraft. These coolers are available for everyone to use now.

Wood for Ovation Guitar

A helicopter company wanted to start making guitars. The company used NASA technology to study vibration. This technology helped them make a guitar with more vibration and better sound.

Cable Car

NASA helped with the grip between the cable and the grip to reduce friction and wear on the cable. This makes it cost less to take care of.
